It's time to get serious

Scientific software is barely more pleasant to use than two decades ago. These days, we have the Internet, virtualisation and cloud computing. It's time to modernise your workflow, and stop wasting your precious time and money.

Instant Execution

No more banging your head against arcane install procedures and inconsistent command-line interfaces. Simmer presents all software with familiar HTML forms.

High Performance, Low Maintenance

Simmer will take care of all your computing needs, running your jobs across a cluster of modern servers.

Easy To Publish

Publishing a piece of software requires only two simple files, and no changes to the original source code.


Supporting Open Science

Simmer proudly supports the Open Science community.
